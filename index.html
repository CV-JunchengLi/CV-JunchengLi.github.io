Biography

I'm a Ph.D. student at the School of Computer Science and Technology, East China Normal University (ECNU), working with Prof. Guixu Zhang and Prof. Faming Fang.
I will graduate in 2021.07.

Research Interests

  • Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Image Processing.
  • Image restoration and enhancement, such as image super-resolution, image denoising, image dehazing, image demosaicing, image deblurring, and low-light image enhancement.
  • Also focus on medical image processing, image stitching, image compression, and image decolorization.
