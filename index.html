Biography

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Center for Mathematical Artificial Intelligence, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, co-advised by Prof. ZENG Tieyong and Prof. Ronald Lok Ming LUI.

I received the Ph.D. degree from the School of Computer Science and Technology, East China Normal University (ECNU), China, in 2021, co-advised by Prof. Guixu Zhang and Prof. Faming Fang.

My research interests include artificial intelligence and its applications to computer vision (e.g., image stitching, image segmentation, and crowd counting) and image processing (e.g., image super-resolution, image denoising, image dehazing, and medical image reconstruction).

This web used to record my research achievement and also used to record my life experience.
